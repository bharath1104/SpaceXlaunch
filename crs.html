    AUGUST 29, 2021

    CRS-23 MISSION

    On Sunday, August 29 at 3:14 a.m. EDT Falcon 9 launched Dragon on the 23rd Commercial Resupply Services (CRS-23) mission from Launch Complex 39A (LC-39A) at Kennedy Space Center, Florida.

    Following stage separation, Falcon 9’s first stage landed on the “A Shortfall of Gravitas” droneship located in the Atlantic Ocean. Dragon separated from Falcon 9’s second stage about twelve minutes after liftoff and will autonomously dock to the space station on Monday, August 30.
